WHAT WE DO

Why data?

The digital age has brought about vast amount of data that can be mined, analyzed, understood and utilized to transform our society. The power of big data analytics can be used to formulate government policies, fight climate change, hunger and diseases and to improve student performance in schools as well as boost profits of businesses. However, most organizations in Africa have yet to tap the benefits that emanate from this data revolution.

Harnessing the power of data analytics

We utilize big data analytics to guide the government in policy implementation

We conduct research to create new data analytical methods and discover new knowledge

We promote cloud-based big data storage and analytics

We educate teachers, students and professionals to help them incorporate data analytics into their current skillset

We collaborate with organizations to provide solutions for their pressing business needs
